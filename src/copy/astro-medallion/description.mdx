Astro Medallion is a planetary clock. It uses different colored LEDs to 
to show the position of planets around the Earth by what astrological sign they are in.

The clock itself is made up of a bare, custom designed, circuit board mounted on wood
frame laser cut by Pagoda Arts in San Francisco and hand finished. It's designed to
stand up on its own with the easel piece (like a picture frame) or be hung up on the 
wall with the built in nail slots.

The Earth is at the center and the planet LEDs extending radially outward show how far
away each planet is from Earth.

The ring of lights on the outside show the current currently visible horizon from your
position on Earth and they move counter-clockwise with the earth's rotation. So any 
planet inside the lit area is visible to you. For example you can track sunrise by seeing
when the horizon lights begin to cross the sign with the Sun. As the lights rotate
and lights are leaving the sign with the Sun that's Sunset.
