
# [Moon and the phases](#moon)

The Moon rotates around the Earth about every 27 days, so the moon on Astro Medallion will traverse an
astrological sign every couple days: 27 days per 12 signs = 2.25 days per sign. This is the fastest 
"planet" on Astro Medallion! You can even see whe the moon is subtly closer or farther from Earth 
by noticing how long it takes to traverse a sign. The longer it takes, the farther from earth. 
It's subtle, but it's fun to notice!

## The Phases

You can tell the phases of the moon by observing the angle between the Sun, Earth, and Moon on 
Astro Medallion! When the Moon and Earth make a 90° angle with the Sun then the Moon will be half full.
When the Moon is opposite of the Sun it's full, and when the Moon is on the same side as the Sun then
it's New!
