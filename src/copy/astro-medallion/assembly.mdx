# [Assembly](#assembly)

When you receive Astro Medallion, the circuit board will be packaged separately from the frame
(in the same box) and you will have to do some simple assembly to mount the circuit board.

### Components:

- 4 x M3 screw
- 4 x M3 washer
- Circuit Board
- 4 x frame standoffs (already attached)

### Instructions

1. Align the four mounting holes on the circuit board with the brass standoffs
   in the frame 
2. Screw in each screw through a washer (so the washer is on the
   top of the circuit board), but only partially
3. Ensure all the screws and
   washers are aligned before lightly tightening them all. 
4. Use a screwdriver to
   lightly tighten the screws ( or with just your fingers is probably sufficient!)
