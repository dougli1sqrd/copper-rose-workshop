# [Description](#description)

- Astro Medallion shows the position of the planets in the sky with respect to the signs of the Zodiac.

- Each lit LED is a different planet represented by a color. The spoke shows what astrological sign the planet is in, and the rung represents how far from earth the planet is.
    <PlanetColors/>

- The ring of warm white LEDs shows your current horizon based on your location, showing what portion of the sky you can see.

- The main display is a custom designed circuit board mounted in a hand-finished laser-cut cherry wood frame (cut by [Pagoda Arts in San Francisco](https://www.pagodaarts.com/)) with brass hardware. It can stand on the hinged easel or hung up on the wall.

- Planet LEDs flashes as a planet crosses an astrological sign threshold, blinking faster as it crosses into the next sign

- Planet colors slowly fade into each other if they occupy the same LED

- Updates every 5 minutes
