## About Me

I've been tutoring for over a year professionally at C2 Education. I meet the kids where they're at and build understanding to bridge any gaps and help them feel confident. I feel like a lot of kids often have at least a partial understanding of something but what they really need is the confidence to try out and use the knowledge that's in there. They often don't realize what they know! So I also see a lot of my job as teaching them confidence in their own abilities and to do research on their own. I've seen my students grow in their abilities and confidence!

I became a tutor after being laid off from my tech job followed by some soul searching. I've found that I really love teaching and this has felt much more fulfilling than tech did for quite some time!
